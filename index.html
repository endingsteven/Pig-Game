Welcome to the Pig-Game

There are 2 Players who are rolling a dice. A Player can roll the dice as much as he whants to build up his Current Score. By hitting the Hold Button, the Current Score will be saved in the Points. Whenever the Player throws a 1 on the dice, the Current Score will be set back to 0 and the Player will be switched. The Player who reaches 100 Points will be the winner of the game.
